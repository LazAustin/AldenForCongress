On the Issues

With liberty and justice for all...

Criminal Justice

As a first responder, Alden works closely with law enforcement on a regular basis. By legalizing cannabis at the federal level, ending qualified immunity, and ending the practice of civil asset forfeiture, law enforcement would be able to focus on more serious issues.

Taxes

Alden supports ending the state income tax, among other taxes. The government taxes us at every turn. This results in a poorer society, as a whole. By cutting frivolous and wasteful government spending, we could work towards slashing taxes.

2nd Amendment

Alden fully supports repealing the NFA and abolishing the ATF. Prohibition only encourages a thriving black market. Rights are not just privileges. Felons who have paid their debt to society and pose no threat to the general public deserve to be able to protect their families via firearm ownership.

Jobs

The best way to grow jobs is for government to get out of the way. Taxes, overcriminalization, extreme regulation, and professional licensing all reduce opportunity and prevent job growth. When becoming a barber requires twice as many hours as becoming a police officer, is it any wonder nobody can get a job?
